About Our Restaurant


Welcome to "Kabli Palao Delights," a haven of Afghan cuisine that will transport your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Kabul. Step inside and immerse yourself in the rich aromas of traditional spices and fragrant rice that fill the air. Our restaurant takes pride in serving the renowned and authentic Kabli Palao, a signature Afghan dish that is a true culinary masterpiece. Each grain of saffron-infused rice is meticulously cooked to perfection, layered with tender, succulent lamb or chicken, caramelized carrots, and a medley of aromatic spices. The flavors unfold with every bite, leaving a lasting impression that will keep you craving for more. At Kabli Palao Delights, we strive to create an inviting atmosphere, where the warm hospitality of Afghanistan is reflected in our attentive service. Whether you're seeking a taste of home or an adventurous culinary experience, our restaurant promises to deliver an unforgettable dining journey that showcases the true essence of Afghan cuisine.
